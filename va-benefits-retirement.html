Resources & FAQ

VA Math & Combined Ratings

The VA uses a combined rating table rather than simple addition. For example, two 50% ratings do not equal 100%; they equal 75%, which rounds to 80%. Our briefing explains the "Efficiency" method used in these calculations.

Concurrent Receipt (CRDP vs CRSC)

CRDP allows retirees with a 50%+ rating to receive both full military retirement and VA disability. CRSC is for combat-related disabilities and is tax-exempt. Eligibility criteria and application steps vary significantly.
